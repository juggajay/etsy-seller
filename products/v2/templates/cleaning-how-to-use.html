How to Use Your Cleaning Business Invoice

Quick setup guide for cleaning professionals

1 Getting Started
  • This PDF has two pages: an Invoice (page 2) and a Quote (page 3). Use whichever you need.
  • Open in Microsoft Edge, Adobe Acrobat Reader (free), or Chrome. All fields and auto-calculations work across viewers.
  • Click on any highlighted field to start typing. Blue-highlighted fields are editable; grey fields calculate automatically.
  • To add your logo: in Adobe Acrobat, go to Edit PDF → Add Image and position over the "Your Logo" area.
2 Filling In Your Invoice
  • Service Details: Enter the service address (client’s property), date of service, and service type (e.g., Deep Clean, Regular, Move-In/Out).
  • Services Section: Enter each room or area cleaned, hours spent, and your hourly rate. The Amount column auto-calculates (hours × rate).
  • Supplies Section: List cleaning products used, quantity, and unit cost. Amount auto-calculates (qty × cost).
  • Tax: Default is 10%. Change the rate if needed — tax amount recalculates automatically.
  • Discount: Default is 0%. Enter a percentage for recurring client discounts or promotions — auto-deducted from the total.
  • Schedule: Note the recurring frequency (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly) and next service date for regular clients.
3 Using the Quote Page
  • Page 3 is a matching Quote template with the same services and supplies layout.
  • Send professional quotes and invoices that match — builds trust with new clients.
  • Quote totals include tax but no discount (discounts apply to invoices for existing clients).
  • Set the validity period (default: 30 days) and describe the scope of work in the footer.
Cleaning Business Tips

Save a blank copy: Always keep an unedited version as your master template.
Recurring clients: Use the schedule section to track weekly/bi-weekly/monthly service dates.
Discounts: Offer 5–10% for recurring clients. Set to 0% for one-time jobs.
File → Save As: Save each completed invoice with a unique name (e.g., INV-001-ClientName.pdf).
