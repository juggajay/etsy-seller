## Key Patterns

### Formats Offered:
- Fillable PDF: ████████████ ALL sellers
- Canva template: ███ ~30% of sellers (GROWING)
- Google Sheets: █ ~10% of sellers (RARE — opportunity)
- Word/Excel: ██ ~20% of sellers
- Multiple formats: ██ ~20% (but these tend to be top performers)

### Auto-Calculate:
- Only ~20-30% of fillable invoice templates have auto-calculating fields
- This is a GENUINE differentiator — most are just text-field forms

### Colour Variants:
- 1 colour: ~50% of sellers
- 2-3 colours: ~30%
- 4+ colours: ~10% (our 4-variant plan puts us in top tier)

### Sizing:
- US Letter only: ~60%
- US Letter + A4: ~30% (better — international buyers exist)
- A4 only: ~10%

### Star Seller Status:
- Most top-ranking listings have Star Seller badge
- Requires: 5-star reviews + fast response time + shipping on time
- We need this badge ASAP for credibility

### Niche Specialization:
- Generic "invoice template" = VERY competitive
- Industry-specific = MUCH less competition
- Examples working: therapist invoices, cleaning service invoices
- Opportunity: construction invoices, freelancer invoices, photographer invoices

## Our Competitive Position

### What We Match:
- ✅ Fillable PDF (table stakes)
- ✅ Multiple colour variants (4 = above average)
- ✅ Auto-calculating fields (top 20-30% of sellers)
- ✅ US Letter + A4 sizing

### What We Beat Them On:
- 🔥 Triple format: PDF + Canva + Google Sheets (almost nobody offers all 3)
- 🔥 4-page premium template (How to Use + Invoice + Terms + Thank You)
- 🔥 Auto-calculate (only 20-30% of competitors have this)
- 🔥 Lower launch price ($3.49 vs typical $4.99-7.99)
- 🔥 Format stacking perceived value

### What We're Weak On:
- ❌ Zero reviews (death valley)
- ❌ No Star Seller badge
- ❌ New store trust signals
- ❌ Visual polish may be below designer-level

### Strategy to Win:
1. Price LOW ($3.49) to drive initial sales despite zero reviews
2. Offer more formats than anyone (PDF + Canva + Sheets)
3. Lead with auto-calculate as headline feature
4. Target niche variations FAST (construction, freelancer, photographer invoices)
5. Build to Star Seller badge ASAP (respond fast, deliver quality)
