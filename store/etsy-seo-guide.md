# Etsy SEO Guide — 2026 Rules

## Title Rules (NEW — Updated 2025/2026)
- **Short and clear.** Under 15 words. Say what the item IS.
- **Front-load key details:** what it is + top 3 descriptors (format, use case, key feature)
- **NO filler words** in titles — "perfect," "beautiful," "unique" go in descriptions/tags only
- **NO sales/shipping info** — Etsy adds this automatically
- **NO keyword stuffing** — don't repeat words
- **Move aspirational terms to tags:** "gift for him," "birthday present" = tags, not title

### Example Title Formula for Our Products:
`[What It Is] [Key Format] [Key Use Case] [Size/Detail]`

**Invoice Template:**
`Fillable Invoice Template PDF | Small Business Invoice | Editable Digital Download`

**NOT:** `Invoice Template Fillable PDF Editable Invoice Form Business Invoice Printable Invoice Small Business Form Digital File Instant Download`

## Tags (13 per listing — USE ALL 13)
- Multi-word phrases, not single words
- Match what buyers actually search for
- Include variations: "invoice template," "billing template," "business invoice"
- Include format tags: "fillable PDF," "editable template," "digital download"
- Include use-case tags: "small business," "freelancer," "entrepreneur"

## Algorithm Ranking Factors (2026)
1. **Query matching** — title, tags, categories, attributes must match search term
2. **Listing quality score** — CTR, add-to-cart rate, favorites, purchases compound over time
3. **Shop quality score** — reviews, fulfillment consistency, response time affect ALL listings
4. **Personalization** — Etsy tailors results per buyer (browsing history, price sensitivity)
5. **Price + shipping** — competitive pricing and free/low shipping improve ranking
6. **New listing boost** — new listings get temporary visibility boost for testing
7. **Meaningful updates** — major changes (new photos, rewritten title) trigger re-evaluation. Minor tag shuffles do nothing.

## Key Insight for New Store
- 46% of Etsy purchases happen via mobile app — thumbnails MUST look good at small size
- New listings get a testing boost — first impressions matter enormously
- Listing quality score compounds — a strong first week of CTR/favorites sets trajectory
- Focus on long-tail keywords with less competition initially

## Competition Analysis: Invoice Templates on Etsy

### What Top Sellers Offer:
- Fillable PDF (Adobe Reader compatible) — STANDARD
- Canva editable version — INCREASINGLY COMMON
- Multiple colour variants (2-4 colours)
- Star Seller badges + thousands of reviews
- Professional mockup images showing template on devices
- "Instant Download" prominently featured

### Our Differentiation:
1. **Auto-calculating fields** — most fillable PDFs have text fields only, no math
2. **Triple format** — PDF + Canva + Google Sheets (most offer 1-2 formats)
3. **4 colour variants** included (many offer 1-3)
4. **Lower launch price** ($2.99-3.99 vs competitor $4.99-7.99)
5. **How-to-use guide** included (reduces support, increases perceived value)

### Niche Opportunities Within Invoice Templates:
- "Mental health invoice template" — specialised, less competition
- "Construction invoice template" — Jay's industry knowledge
- "Freelancer invoice template" — high search volume
- Industry-specific invoices have LESS competition than generic "invoice template"
